Chocolate Muffin

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Mix together flour, sugar, eggs and milk then stir until it combines nicely and become smooth.
  2. Add chocolate powder with a bit of water and stir one more time until the mixture gets chocolate brown coloured.
  3. Bake for 35 min and keep on checking to that it does not get thick but becomes tender and soft.
